About us

Welcome to aster, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of tizi-ouzou, our restaurant is a heaven for food lovers seeking a memorable dining experience. Since 2017, we've been proud to serve the vibrant community of tizi-ouzou. Thank you for choosing aster. We look forward to sharing our passion for food and hospitality with you. Bon Appétit!
